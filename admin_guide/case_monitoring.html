Case Monitoring & Management
Case Overview Dashboard

The Case Monitoring section allows you to oversee all legal cases on the platform. Access it from the main sidebar by clicking on Cases.

Case Status Types:
Active (in progress)
Pending (awaiting action)
Completed (resolved)
Disputed (requires attention)
Case Management Actions
View Case Details

Click on any case to view complete information including client details, lawyer assignment, case documents, and communication history.

Reassign Lawyer

If needed, you can reassign a case to a different lawyer by clicking the "Reassign" button in the case details page.

Flag for Review

Mark cases requiring special attention with the "Flag" button. Flagged cases appear in the priority queue.

Send Notifications

Contact clients or lawyers directly through the platform messaging system from the case details page.

Case Distribution
Case Monitoring Best Practices
  • Regular Review: Check the dashboard daily for new cases and status changes.
  • Prioritize Disputes: Address disputed cases within 24 hours to maintain client satisfaction.
  • Monitor Aging Cases: Pay special attention to cases that have been active for more than 30 days.
  • Quality Assurance: Randomly review completed cases to ensure service quality standards.
  • Documentation: Ensure all case actions and administrative interventions are properly documented.

Common Resolution Actions
Reassign Lawyer: Transfer the case to a different lawyer.
Partial Refund: Process a partial refund for services not satisfactorily delivered.
Full Refund: Process a complete refund in cases of significant service failure.

Best Practices for Dispute Resolution
Remain Neutral

Maintain impartiality when mediating disputes. Consider both sides equally.

Respond Promptly

Acknowledge new disputes within 24 hours. Aim to resolve within 7 days.

Document Everything

Maintain detailed records of all communications, evidence, and actions taken.

Track Patterns

Monitor for recurring issues to identify systemic problems that need addressing.
